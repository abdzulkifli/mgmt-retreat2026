Recommended Session Format

Each strategic session is designed as a working session. Participants do not merely listen; they collectively develop, test and validate the roadmap.

15–20 minPresent current situation, evidence and existing proposals.
20–30 minChallenge assumptions and identify strategic gaps.
20–30 minComplete the roadmap template collectively.
10–15 minValidate initiatives, milestones, targets and ownership.
5 minCPS summarises agreed outputs and unresolved matters.
Role clarity: CPS provides the structure, facilitates the discussion, coordinates alignment and consolidates the outputs. Ideas, targets, decisions, ownership, accountability and subsequent execution remain with the respective Heads of Division and Managers.
